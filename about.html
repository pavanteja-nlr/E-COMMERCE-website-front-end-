About Vibhav Restro

Welcome to Vibhav Restro, your family's favorite place to dine and celebrate!

Our Story

Vibhav Restro was founded with a passion for bringing families and friends together over delicious food in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our journey began in 2024, and since then, we have been committed to serving authentic flavors, quality ingredients, and memorable experiences.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Wide variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes
  • Signature biryanis and mouth-watering starters
  • Fresh, locally sourced ingredients
  • Friendly staff and family-friendly ambiance
  • Customizable party and event menus

Our Mission

To create a place where every meal is a celebration and every guest feels at home.
